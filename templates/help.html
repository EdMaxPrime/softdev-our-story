Welcome to Our Story!

At the top there is a menubar. Use it to navigate the site.
  • All Stories lists all the stories we have. Checkout the cool search filter.
  • The search box lets you search by title
  • My Page is your profile! Access every story you created, modified, or liked
  • New Story is where you get to make your own stories!
Here are some links you may see on this website:
  • Blue links: these are links to stories. Click on them to see/edit a story.
  • Pink links: these are links to user profile pages. Click on them to see the profile.
  • Green links: these are genres. Click on them to search for that genre.
If you see a story you want to come back to or support, click "like" when viewing it. It will be public on your profile so you can go back to it.
Most stories are accessible to those without accounts. You can send the link in the URL to friends who, for reasons unknown, aren't already using this great website.
When viewing a story, hover over words to see who contributed them
